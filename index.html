ABOUT US

Welcome to Elite Events Chicago. We are the event planners of choice for clients who want a genuinely bespoke event, delivered flawlessly. We deliver events across the Chicago area and beyond from our headquarters in the city.




Welcome to Elite Events Chicago. We are renowned for the creativity of our events as well as our extreme attention to detail, even for very large and complex events. We aspire to make all our clients feel as if they are our only client. Clients select us because our event planning approach and our commercial model are different to most of our competitors. With years of event planning experience as bespoke event and wedding designers, we create unique, luxury parties tailored perfectly to your personal style. Elite Events Chicago prides itself on world-class service, deep event planning experience, and the stunning events we create. Our team of experienced event planners and creative designers work alongside clients to create unique experiences.
